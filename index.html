About Us

DevSharks

DevSharks is a vibrant and pioneering student community dedicated to fostering innovation and collaboration in the realm of technology. Renowned for orchestrating a series of groundbreaking hackathons, both online and offline, our community has emerged as a beacon of creativity and problem-solving prowess.
With an illustrious history of partnering with esteemed institutions, DevSharks has seamlessly blended the academic prowess of leading institutes with the dynamic energy of our community. These collaborations have not only elevated the quality of our events but have also amplified our impact within the tech sphere.
Our hackathons stand as a testament to our commitment, drawing in over 5000 participants from diverse corners of the globe. We take immense pride in providing a platform where budding innovators and seasoned tech enthusiasts converge, ideate, and transform ideas into tangible solutions.

What sets DevSharks apart is our unwavering dedication to nurturing talent, fostering a culture of continuous learning, and encouraging out-of-the-box thinking. Our community thrives on the diversity of perspectives, fostering an environment where innovation knows no bounds.
Join us in our mission to push the boundaries of technology, where every line of code, every idea, and every collaboration contributes to a greater technological landscape. DevSharks invites you to be part of an extraordinary journey, where innovation, learning, and community are at the forefront of our endeavors..

What We Do at DevSharks

Our student branch is passionate about community engagement of students and provides a networking opportunity to meet and learn from fellow students, as well as from faculty and professional members in the field of their interests, professions, and ideas. We have started a Women in Engineering Group (WIE-G) and are also planning to introduce a computer society (CS) chapter.
We, at our student branch, enhance the degree of importance of providing guidance for students and also aim to create a clear image of various available options in moulding a successful career. The executive committee members volunteering for our student branch are young budding engineers along with trained professors, and many more goal-driven people. We work together to carry out educational and focussed activities like technical events, Seminars, Workshops, Webinars, and also activities that foster social responsibilities. We are supportive and creative to novel ideas that link up with the latest technologies and are looking forward to reaching greater heights.
